I am currently a fifth‑year Ph.D. candidate in the interdisciplinary Algorithms, Combinatorics, and Optimization (ACO) doctoral program at the Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. R. Ravi and Prof. Benjamin Moseley. My research focuses on optimization under uncertainty, particularly through online and learning‑augmented algorithms.
